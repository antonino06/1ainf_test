Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakers the world has never knowm. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and there are their stories.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. MIx eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
